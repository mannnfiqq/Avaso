What is F1 in Schools

F1 in Schools is an international STEM competition where students design, build, and race miniature Formula 1 cars. It fosters creativity, collaboration, and technical skills in a fun and competitive environment.

What is AVASO

AVASO is a dynamic student-led team competing in the F1 in Schools challenge. We combine engineering, design, and teamwork to innovate and push boundaries in STEM education.
